our story


IIIT-Delhi has completed 10 years and Odyssey ‘18 has established us as a competitive name as far as college Cultural Festivals are concerned. Taking Odyssey forward, we see what each version before us has created and which venues we still have to improve in. Each year IIIT-Delhi establishes itself more as a brand-name and Odyssey comes up better than the previous year.

This year, Odyssey ‘19 looks to create a feeling which binds all the students of IIIT-Delhi together with the exceptional culture that exists in all Delhi colleges while bringing in the largest crowd any Fest could hope to see. We see the entire workforce of IIIT-Delhi, our army of volunteers and organizers, working towards one common goal: creating an Odyssey that IIIT-Delhi is proud of.

The face of a Fest are its ambience, events and the star night. To build up these three facets, we will have strong pillars of sponsorship, public relations, publicity and infrastructure supporting the Fest. This year, Odyssey ‘19 will require dedicated research into building a successful Fest. We will brainstorm upon each aspect of the Fest and will combine our experience with learnings from how other Fests are organized. Each team will have an end vision which will be aimed towards creating the Odyssey ‘19 we wish to achieve. With this aim in mind, we will work towards creating a Fest which will float the name of Odyssey into the best Cultural College Festivals of Delhi.
